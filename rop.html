Reorder Point Methods and When to Use Them

Constant Demand, Constant Lead Time

Use when both demand and lead time are stable and predictable. No safety stock is needed. Formula: ROP = D * L

Where: D = Average demand, L = Average lead time

Variable Demand, Constant Lead Time

Use when demand fluctuates but lead time is fixed. Includes safety stock for demand variability. Formula: ROP = D * L + Z * σ_d * √L

Where: Z = Z-score for service level, σ_d = Standard deviation of demand

Constant Demand, Variable Lead Time

Use when demand is stable but lead time varies. Includes safety stock for lead time variability. Formula: ROP = D * L + Z * D * σ_L

Where: σ_L = Standard deviation of lead time
