Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your calculators?

Our calculators are designed to provide accurate results based on standard industrial engineering formulas. However, we recommend verifying critical calculations through additional methods.

Can I request a new calculator tool?

Yes! We welcome suggestions for new calculators. Please use the contact form to submit your request.

Do you offer premium or paid versions?

No, all our calculators are completely free to use. We may offer additional features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How can I support this project?

You can support us by sharing our tools with colleagues, providing feedback, or making a donation through our PayPal link on the homepage.

What information should I include in my message?

For technical issues, please include details about your browser, device, and the specific calculator you were using. For suggestions, be as detailed as possible about the functionality you'd like to see.
