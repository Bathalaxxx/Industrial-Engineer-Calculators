About Industrial Engineering Calculators

Our Mission

Industrial Engineering Calculators was founded with a simple goal: to provide free, easy-to-use tools that help industrial engineering professionals and students solve common problems quickly and accurately.

We believe that access to quality engineering tools shouldn't be limited by budget or technical expertise. Our calculators are designed to be intuitive yet powerful, helping you focus on your work rather than manual calculations.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of industrial engineering professionals, this project began as a collection of spreadsheet tools we used in our daily work. As we shared these tools with colleagues, we realized there was a need for a centralized, web-based platform that would make these calculations accessible to everyone.

What started as a side project has grown into a comprehensive resource used by thousands of engineers worldwide. We're constantly adding new calculators and improving existing ones based on user feedback.
