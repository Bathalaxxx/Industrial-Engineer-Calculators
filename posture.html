Practical Examples

Office Worker

Assembly Line Worker

REBA: Score 7 (Medium risk) - Redesign workstation layout

OWAS: Category 3 - Changes needed as soon as possible

Construction Worker

REBA: Score 11 (Very high risk) - Immediate intervention needed

OWAS: Category 4 - Immediate corrective actions
