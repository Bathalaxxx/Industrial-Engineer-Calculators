Safety Stock Methods and When to Use Them

Constant Demand, Constant Lead Time

Use when both demand and lead time are stable and predictable. Typically, no safety stock is needed, but a minimal buffer may be set for unexpected disruptions. Formula: SS = 0 (or minimal buffer based on service level).

Variable Demand, Constant Lead Time

Use when demand fluctuates but lead time is fixed. Accounts for demand variability during lead time. Formula: SS = Z * σ_d * √L

Where: Z = Z-score for service level, σ_d = Standard deviation of demand, L = Average lead time

Constant Demand, Variable Lead Time

Use when demand is stable but lead time varies. Accounts for lead time variability. Formula: SS = Z * D * σ_L

Where: D = Average demand, σ_L = Standard deviation of lead time
