5S System Audit

5S is a workplace organization method that uses a list of five Japanese words: Seiri (Sort), Seiton (Set in order), Seiso (Shine), Seiketsu (Standardize), and Shitsuke (Sustain). This checklist helps you audit and improve your 5S implementation.
