About the EOQ Model

The Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) model determines the optimal order quantity that minimizes the total inventory costs, which include ordering costs and holding costs. This calculator helps you find the most cost-effective quantity to order based on your specific inventory parameters.

When to Use Basic EOQ

Use the basic EOQ model when:

  • You're ordering from suppliers with no quantity discounts
  • Stockouts are not allowed or are extremely costly
  • Demand is constant and known
  • Lead time is constant and known

Example: A retail store ordering standard items with consistent pricing where running out of stock would mean lost sales.
