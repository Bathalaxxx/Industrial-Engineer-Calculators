About EOQ

The Economic Order Quantity model determines the optimal order quantity that minimizes total inventory costs (ordering + holding costs).

Basic EOQ Example

A retailer sells 10,000 units/year of a product with $50 ordering cost and $2.50 holding cost/unit. The EOQ helps determine how many units to order at a time to minimize total costs.
