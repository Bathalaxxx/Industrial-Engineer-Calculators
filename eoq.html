Interpretation & Recommendation

When to Use Each Variation:

Basic EOQ

Use for standard inventory items with constant demand and no quantity discounts. Example: Office supplies ordered monthly.

Quantity Discount EOQ

Use when suppliers offer price breaks for larger orders. Example: Bulk purchasing of raw materials where unit cost decreases after 500 units.

Production Order Quantity

Use when items are produced internally rather than ordered. Example: Manufacturing batches where production rate exceeds demand rate.
