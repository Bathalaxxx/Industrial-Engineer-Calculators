EOQ Variations and When to Use Them

Basic EOQ

Use when inventory is replenished instantly, and no shortages or discounts are allowed. Formula: EOQ = √(2DS/H)

Where: D = Annual Demand, S = Ordering Cost, H = Holding Cost

EOQ with Backordering

Use when shortages are allowed, and backorders are fulfilled later. Incorporates backordering costs. Formula: EOQ = √(2DS/H) * √((H + B)/B), where B = Backordering Cost

EOQ with Quantity Discounts

Use when suppliers offer price discounts for ordering larger quantities. Compares total costs with and without discounts.
