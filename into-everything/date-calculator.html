Frequently Asked Questions — Date Calculator

How do I calculate the number of days between two dates?

Select start and end dates, then click Calculate. The result shows total days and a breakdown of years, months, and days.

Does it account for leap years and different month lengths?

Yes — the calculator uses real calendar arithmetic, so February 29 on leap years is handled correctly.

How can I calculate only working days?

Use the "Calculate in business days" checkbox when adding or subtracting days — weekends are skipped automatically.
