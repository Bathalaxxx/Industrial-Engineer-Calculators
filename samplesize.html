Practical Examples

Quality Control Example

Estimating defect rate in a production batch:

Population: 10,000 | Estimated Defect Rate: 2% | Confidence: 95% | Margin: ±1%

Sample Size Needed: 753 (without FPC) or 690 (with FPC)

Process Improvement Example

Measuring average processing time:

Std Dev: 2.5 minutes | Confidence: 95% | Margin: ±0.5 minutes

Sample Size Needed: 96 observations

Employee Survey Example

Surveying satisfaction in a small company:

Population: 150 employees | Confidence: 95% | Margin: ±5%

Sample Size Needed: 108 employees
